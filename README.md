## What is Grammarly?

Grammarly is a sophisticated writing assistant that elevates the quality and precision of text through real-time linguistic intelligence. It evaluates wording, structure, tone, and clarity, helping authors produce writing that is polished and aligned with their intended message. Whether you're drafting long-form content, refining business communication, or preparing academic material, Grammarly analyzes your text holistically and offers actionable suggestions that improve readability and impact.

The desktop application provides a focused environment for working with documents, combining editing tools with a responsive AI engine that interprets context and intent. Grammarly identifies inconsistencies, structural weaknesses, imprecise phrasing, and stylistic issues, then proposes revisions tailored to the purpose of your writing. Its versatility makes it suitable for professional correspondence, content creation, research writing, internal documentation, and everyday communication.

Grammarly’s ecosystem extends across browsers, text editors, and productivity suites, ensuring consistent writing support wherever you choose to work. The platform synchronizes documents, maintains user-specific preferences, and adapts recommendations to personal writing patterns. For teams and organizations, Grammarly contributes to unified communication standards and consistent messaging.
